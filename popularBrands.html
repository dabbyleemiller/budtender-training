Green Life Productions

Based in Pahrump, Nevada, Green Life Productions, or GLP, is known best for their organic, no-till-soil growing methods. Rather than use pesticides, they use preying mantises as pest control. They also use a network of mushrooms and their mycelium network to transfer nutrients throughout the soil.

Most Popular Products

Organic Flower and Bottom Buds are this company's bread and butter, and are often dropped in small batches.

Rosin is another popular product they make, solventless concentrate using only heat and pressure to extract cannabinoids and terpenes

Baby J's are small. .3g prerolled joints that come in a cute little Altoids-type tin.

Check out their website

Wyld Gummies

By far the most popular brand of gummies, they have a distinctly pleasant texture and relatively zero cannabis taste. They are also consistent in effect and their ratioed gummies, those that include CBD, CBN, CBC, or CBG, are also very effective.

Check out their website

Medizin, Trendi, Haha, and Dreamland

These are the major brands of the behemouth Planet 13. They also have a smaller local shop called Medizin. But they are, as to my knowledge, the largest dispensary in the world, and just added their consumption lounge that I believe just opened. They have many signature strains and mammoth production ability.

Medizin Flower is the cornerstone, under which they also have concentrates, most notably their live resin. They also do cartridges and prerolls

Trendi is their more experimental brand, focusing also on concetrates and vapes.

Haha Gummies and Dreamland Chocolate are both made onsite and are the edible side of their product line.

Check out their website

Nature's Chemistry

Nature's Chem is generally known for its high-potency flower and its high terpenes. They have great signature strains such as Ghost Train Haze, Blue Hash Plant, and Garlic Cookies.
